\fbox{\parbox{\dimexpr\linewidth-2\fboxsep-2\fboxrule}{
20-4.
Use the result of Problems 20-2 and 17-5 to
show $\langle Q_1\rangle$, $\langle Q_2\rangle$
and $\langle P_1\rangle$, $\langle P_2\rangle$
are zero for any state where the quantity represented
by $L_3$ has a definite value.
}}

\bigskip
From problem 20-2 we have
\begin{align*}
Q_1L_3-L_3Q_1&=-i\hbar Q_2
\\
Q_2L_3-L_3Q_2&=i\hbar Q_1
\end{align*}

and
\begin{align*}
P_1L_3-L_3P_1&=-i\hbar P_2
\\
P_2L_3-L_3P_2&=i\hbar P_1
\end{align*}

From problem 17-5 we have for when $L_3$ has a definite value
\begin{equation*}
\langle -i\hbar Q_2\rangle=0,\quad
\langle i\hbar Q_1\rangle=0,\quad
\langle -i\hbar P_2\rangle=0,\quad
\langle i\hbar P_1\rangle=0
\end{equation*}
